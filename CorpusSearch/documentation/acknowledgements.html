CorpusSearch was written by Beth Randall as part of a project at the University of Pennsylvania, directed by Anthony Kroch, to create large parsed corpora of historical English. The program design is due to Beth Randall, Anthony Kroch, and Ann Taylor.

The users guide is being maintained by Beatrice Santorini.
