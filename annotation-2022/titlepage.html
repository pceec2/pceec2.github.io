Annotation manual for the Parsed Corpus of Early English Correspondence 2

Beatrice Santorini
(February 2022)

This annotation manual is the latest revision of previous versions (2004, 2016). It is heavily indebted to the guidelines developed by Ann Taylor and Tony Kroch for Middle English (Kroch and Taylor 2000) as well as to the spirit of the guidelines for the Penn Treebank (Marcus, Santorini, and Marcinkiewicz 1993). The substance of the guidelines remains largely unchanged, but certain aspects of the annotation scheme have been streamlined, as set out in Changes.

The 2022 guidelines are in force for the following corpus:

  • Parsed Corpus of Early English Correspondence 2 (PCEEC2)
It is planned to extend them to the following corpora at their next release:
  • Penn Parsed Corpora of Historical English (Kroch 2020)
    • Penn-Helsinki Parsed Corpus of Middle English, 2nd edition (PPCME2) (Kroch and Taylor 2000)
    • Penn-Helsinki Parsed Corpus of Early Modern English (PPCEME) (Kroch, Santorini, and Delfs 2004)
    • Penn Parsed Corpus of Modern British English, 2nd edition (PPCMBE2) (Kroch, Santorini, and Diertani 2016)
