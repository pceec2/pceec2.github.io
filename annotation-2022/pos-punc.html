Dashes

Dashes in the original text are represented by hyphens in the annotated corpus. At token boundaries, they go with the first token if they are the only punctuation at the token boundary. 
He is finished with the job -/PUNC

or so he says .
Otherwise, they go with the second token. 
He is finished with the job ,/PUNC

-/PUNC or so he says .

Periods

Periods that are not used as sentential punctuation are not separated from the word they belong to. These include periods indicating abbreviation and surrounding numbers and certain words (e.g., .x., .Mon.).
